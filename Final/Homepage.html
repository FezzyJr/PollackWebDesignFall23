Roald Dahl

Website by: Carlos Pollack

Have you ever heard of Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory? Or maybe even James in the Giant Peach? Well, those books are very popular and known worldwide. Most of his books were made into movies, like Willy Wonka and the Chocolate Factory, BFG, and many more!

You may have had to read a book or 2 of his for a book report when you were younger. His books are very easy to understand and most of his books are made for children to read. In 1983 he won World Fantasy Award for Life Achievement and the British Book Awards' Children's Author of the Year in 1990. He is one of the most top rated childrens author of all time. Most of the books he wrote are bestsellers. You should read some and go find out for yourself. If you want to read more about him, click the links at the bottom of this page.
